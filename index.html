About 3D_model.co

3D_model.co is a curated gallery of high-quality 3D models for enthusiasts, designers, and developers. We showcase various models from talented creators on Sketchfab and now feature Polycam-captured 3D scans as well.

All models are embedded from their original sources and proper attribution is provided to the original creators. Our gallery continues to grow as we discover new and interesting 3D content worth sharing with our community.
